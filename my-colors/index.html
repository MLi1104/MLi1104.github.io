I used the monochromatic color scheme mostly, but added bits of other colors to complement the purple a little better. I think I was trying to using themes of magic and fantasy, but I also wanted to imitate the colors of lavenders and allow the readers to think of flowers when they look at the website. The purples I used also have more red than blue so it creates a cheerful, happy mood while being calm and relaxed.

Here are the Materials I Need

  • a nice set of acrylic paints
  • a glass of water to rinse brushes
  • nice brushes
  • watercolor paper or canvas
  • paper towels
  • music or a podcast
